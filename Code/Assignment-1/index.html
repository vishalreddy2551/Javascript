SIDDHU MOOSAWALA

1993-2022

Biography

Siddhu Moosawala a.k.a Shubdeep Singh Siddhu was born in Moosa village, punjab.
He made his debut in music industry with his G-Wagon song which was feautred
in global billboard. After this iconic song he moved to his moosa pind where many
people moves to metro cities he stick to his pind. Later he has give
60 iconic hit songs in is 4 year carrier.
He made various hit albums like PBX1, Moosetape, No name, etc.
He won best album award at Brit Asia TV music awards and his Legend
song won Song Of The Year title.
